Project idea 

 

The project idea I have come up is of developing an application to get your medicines home delivered within 30 minutes 

or the medicine price would be given at a discount of 10 %.

 

Overview - The idea behind developing this application would be making it convenient for customers to get their medicines delivered at home with just a click of a button they could just upload a snapshot of there prescription which then would be directed to the nearest medicine retailer to be shipped to the customer . I am also aware there are some medicine delivery application in the market but seeing their review and from my personal experience they are not very prompt with there service.

 

Motivation 

 

The real motivation for building this application would be helping the older customers which can't go out and around looking for the medicines and also for the people who keep busy and having an application which could deliver the medicine in 30 minutes would make life easier for them. Seeing the lifestyle nowadays where technology has a big role in everybody's life and almost everyone uses a smartphone and would love to build an application which would deliver the medicines promptly with great customer satisfaction and helping the community.

Description 

Well, the process of getting these medicines delivered on time would be a challenge and that means the whole process starting from the prescription of the medicine to be uploaded till it reaches the customer has to be prompt and without any loopholes or mistakes.

For this, I have thought of delegating the whole process into three major panels which are the pharmacy panel, customer panel and the admin panel so that there are no confusions and mistakes in the process and if in case of a fault we could know which panel could be made better so that there is no error again.
