    Project idea 

    The project idea I have come up is of developing an application to get your medicines home delivered within 30 minutes, or the medicine price would be given at a discount of 10 %.

    Overview

    The idea behind developing this application would be making it convenient for customers to get their medicines delivered at home with just a click of a button they could just upload a snapshot of there prescription which then would be directed to the nearest medicine retailer to be shipped to the customer . I am also aware there are some medicine delivery application in the market but seeing their review and from my personal experience they are not very prompt with there service.well in this project our prime goal would be a speedy delivery which is like making the whole idea of medicine delivery prompt .

    Motivation 

    The real motivation for building this application would be helping the older customers which can't go out and around looking for the medicines and also for the people who keep busy and having an application which could deliver the medicine in 30 minutes would make life easier for them. Seeing the lifestyle nowadays where technology has a big role in everybody's life and almost everyone uses a smartphone and would love to build an application which would deliver the medicines promptly with great customer satisfaction and helping the community.

    Description 

    Well, the process of getting these medicines delivered on time would be a challenge and that means the whole process starting from the prescription of the medicine to be uploaded till it reaches the customer has to be prompt and without any loopholes or mistakes.

    For this, I have thought of delegating the whole process into three major panels which are the pharmacy panel, customer panel and the admin panel so that there are no confusions and mistakes in the process and if in case of a fault we could know which panel could be made better so that there is no error again. 

    Pharmacy Panel 

  • The customers can sign upto their pharmacy services on the medicine delivery aplication without a glitch with an straightforward-to-use interface.
  • Pharmacists can use a user-friendly interface to handle and generate future orders.
  • Users can get notifications about the drug store purchases they have made.
  • The online medication app provides the ease of evaluating the prescriptions uploaded by the clients digitally.
  • Inside the application, the Stores can receive payments directly and track their details as well.
  • To attract the customer, the pharmacy stores can Interact and engage with them through push notifications
  • Seamlessly add/ alter/ delete store details to give updated information to the customers.

    • Customer Panel 

  • Users can join and log in to the application with their email, contact number, and password.
  • The online medicine delivery application enables the users to maintain their information in the profile panel.
  • Customers can effortlessly add prescriptions as guided by the doctor in the profile panel.
  • Customers can search for specific medicines by inserting the name of the medication with all the important details.

    • Admin Panel 

  • Admin confirms and views all the requests made by customers, store owners, in the window pane .
  • The application enables multiple payment accesses so that payments can be made flawlessly, which also increases the business base.
  • Data can be retrieved in various types by admin, which in this manner facilitates real-time analytics.
  • Admin, and the customers as well, can trace the orders put by customers at a variety of stores also on a real-time basis.
  • Find the best delivery routes using GPS enabled navigation systems to ensure the right-time delivery.
  • Customers get 24/7 assistance during emergency .
